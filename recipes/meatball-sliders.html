Meatball Stuffed Garlic Bread Sliders

Description

These are some unforgettable sliders by Chef John from Food Wishes. Not only are these sliders relatively easy to make, they are a fun way for you to share your delicious creation with your friends and family!

In this recipe, you will learn how to make these incredible, shareable meatball sliders. As stated previously: this recipe is much easier than it seems. Using Hawaiian rolls ensures that all we must deal with is whatever we would like to stuff them with. This recipe will show you how to make meatball sliders, but I have made buffalo chicken and onion sliders using this same method with excellent results, so get creative!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced, divided
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons chopped Italian parsley, plus more for garnish
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup plain bread crumbs
  • 1 cup shredded fontina cheese, divided
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 18 dinner rolls (I like the sweet Hawaiian rolls)
  • 2 cups spicy tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated parmesan
